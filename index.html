Common Noun is a free periodical zine dedicated to bolstering the Detroit creative community.
We believe in the inherent creativity of all individuals, and aim to be a platform for all creatives, no matter their preferred medium or level of experience. We are anti-zionist, anti-racist, queer and immigrant friendly, intersectional feminists. We strive to create an inclusive and accessible space, embracing the diversity of our community, and fostering a sense of shared creative energy here in Detroit.
